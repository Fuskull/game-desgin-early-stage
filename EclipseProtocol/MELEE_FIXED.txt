MELEE SYSTEM - COMPLETELY FIXED
================================

WHAT WAS WRONG:
--------------
- Melee attack returned data but play.lua had to process it
- Complex distance calculations in two places
- Enemies weren't being damaged reliably

THE FIX:
--------
Simplified the entire system:

1. MELEE NOW HANDLES EVERYTHING:
   ✓ Checks for enemies in range directly
   ✓ Applies damage immediately
   ✓ Returns list of killed enemies
   ✓ All logic in one place (weapons.lua)

2. HOW IT WORKS:
   - Click with melee equipped
   - weapons.meleeAttack() calculates attack center
   - Checks all turrets within 60 pixel range
   - Checks all hunters within 60 pixel range
   - Damages them instantly (50 HP)
   - Returns list of killed enemies

3. PLAY.LUA SIMPLIFIED:
   - Just calls weapons.attack()
   - Gets list of killed enemies
   - Spawns pickups for dead enemies
   - Removes dead enemies from lists

MELEE STATS:
-----------
Range: 60 pixels (from player center)
Damage: 50 HP
Cooldown: 0.5 seconds
Visual: White arc swing effect

HOW TO USE:
----------
1. Press Q to switch to melee
2. Click near an enemy
3. If enemy is within 60 pixels, it takes 50 damage
4. Visual swing effect shows
5. Dead enemies drop pickups

TESTING:
-------

✓ Click near hunter - should damage it
