BOSS FIGHT IMPLEMENTATION - LEVEL 10

=== CHANGES MADE ===

1. NEW FILE: boss.lua
   - Created a boss enemy system with two phases
   - Phase 1 (MELEE ONLY): Boss only takes damage from melee attacks (red tint)
   - Phase 2 (GUN ONLY): Boss only takes damage from gun attacks (blue tint)
   - Boss is 2x the size of regular enemies (70x70 vs 35x35)
   - Boss has 500 health (switches phase at 250 health)
   - Boss has a large health bar displayed at top of screen
   - Boss moves faster and shoots more aggressively than regular turrets
   - Boss drops 100 XP, large health pack, and ammo when defeated

2. MODIFIED: main.lua
   - Added require("boss") to load the boss module

3. MODIFIED: world.lua
   - Boss spawns at level 10 instead of regular enemies
   - Door only appears when boss is defeated (along with other enemies)
   - Enemy counter includes boss when active

4. MODIFIED: states/play.lua
   - Initialize boss system on game start
   - Update boss each frame
   - Check bullet hits on boss (gun damage)
   - Check melee hits on boss (melee damage)
   - Boss drops rewards when defeated
   - Draw boss on screen

5. MODIFIED: weapons.lua
   - Reduced starting ammo from 100 to 30
   - Players start with less ammo to make resource management more challenging

6. MODIFIED: pickups.lua
   - Integrated new visual assets for pickups:
     * health.png - Health pack sprite
     * ammo pack.png - Ammo box sprite
     * coin.png - XP/coin sprite
   - Added sprite loading in init() function
   - Updated draw() function to render sprites instead of colored rectangles
   - Added blinking effect when pickups are about to expire (last 3 seconds)
   - Adjusted pickup sizes and scales for better visibility
   - Fallback to colored rectangles if sprites fail to load

=== HOW TO PLAY ===

- Reach level 10 to fight the boss
- Phase 1 (RED): Use MELEE attacks only (press Q to switch to melee)
- Phase 2 (BLUE): Use GUN attacks only (press Q to switch to gun)
- Watch the boss health bar at the top of the screen
- Boss changes phase at 50% health (250/500)
- Defeat the boss to clear level 10 and continue
- Collect pickups that drop from enemies:
  * Health packs (red cross icon) - Restore health
  * Ammo boxes (ammo pack icon) - Restore 30 ammo
  * Coins (gold coin icon) - Gain XP for leveling up

=== BOSS STATS ===

- Health: 500 (250 per phase)
- Size: 2x regular enemy (70x70)
- Speed: 120 (faster than turrets)
- Shoot Cooldown: 1.5 seconds (faster than turrets)
- Bullet Damage: 15 (higher than turrets)
- Range: 400 (longer than turrets)
- Rewards: 100 XP, Large Health Pack, Ammo Pack

- Health packs now display with health.png sprite
