Sound Files Needed for Phase 2
================================

Place the following sound files in this directory:

REQUIRED:
---------
1. hit.wav       - Player takes damage sound
2. dash.wav      - Dash ability sound
3. music.mp3     - Background music (looping)

OPTIONAL (for extra polish):
-----------------------------
4. gameover.wav  - Game over sound
5. victory.wav   - Victory sound
6. collect.wav   - Item collection sound

Where to get free sounds:
-------------------------
- freesound.org
- opengameart.org
- bfxr.net (generate your own)
- sfxr.me (online generator)

The game will run without these files, but sounds will be disabled.
Error messages will appear in console if files are missing.

Tips:
-----
- Keep files small (under 1MB each)
- Use .wav for sound effects (better for short sounds)
- Use .mp3 or .ogg for music (better compression)
- Test volume levels in-game
